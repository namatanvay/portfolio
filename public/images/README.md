# Images Directory Structure

Place your images in the appropriate folders:

## Profile
`profile/` - Your professional headshot/profile photo
- Recommended size: 800x800px or higher
- Format: JPG or PNG

## Portfolio Images

### Portraits
`portfolio/portraits/` - Portrait photography work
- High-resolution images (at least 1920px on longest side)
- Format: JPG (optimized for web)

### Events
`portfolio/events/` - Event photography (weddings, corporate events, etc.)
- High-resolution images
- Format: JPG (optimized for web)

### Landscapes
`portfolio/landscapes/` - Landscape and nature photography
- High-resolution images
- Format: JPG (optimized for web)

### Commercial
`portfolio/commercial/` - Commercial photography work
- High-resolution images
- Format: JPG (optimized for web)

## Client Logos
`clients/` - Client company logos
- Format: PNG with transparent background preferred
- Recommended size: 200x100px or similar aspect ratio

## Image Guidelines
- **Naming**: Use descriptive names (e.g., `wedding-couple-sunset.jpg`)
- **Optimization**: Images will be automatically optimized by Astro
- **Format**: WebP versions will be generated automatically
- **Size**: Original high-res files are fine, they'll be resized appropriately
